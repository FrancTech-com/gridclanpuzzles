About us

Games made in Uganda, for everyone

Founded in 2026, ETHELES STUDIO builds games that respect the player: skill decides who wins, tournaments are never pay-to-win, and everything runs on the devices and networks people actually have — starting with any phone browser.

OF
Ofoyrwoth Francis
Founder & CEO

A student at Makerere University in Kampala, Francis founded ETHELES STUDIO in 2026 to prove that world-class games can be built in Uganda — games that are fair to play, kind to low-end phones, and rewarding for the players who love them.

◆ Skill first

Points, ranks and tournament brackets measure ability, not spending. Nothing you can buy makes you win.

◆ Fair rewards

Players earn reward points through optional ads and redeem them for real mobile-money rewards — transparently, with the value always shown before confirming.

◆ Privacy by design

Minimal data, in-app data export and deletion, and full compliance with the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019.
