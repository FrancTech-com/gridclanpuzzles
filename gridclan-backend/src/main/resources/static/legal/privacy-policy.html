GridClan Puzzles Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

GridClan Puzzles ("we", "us") operates the GridClan Puzzles free-to-play puzzle game platform. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to all users worldwide; jurisdiction-specific sections appear at the end.

We practise data minimisation: we do not collect your real name, home address, or identity documents. We never see or store card numbers — card payments happen on our payment partner's secure page.

2. Payments, reward points and redemptions

  • Reward points: watching an advertisement credits reward points to your in-app wallet. Reward points can be redeemed for the mobile-money value shown in the app. We record each completed ad view (time, points credited) to credit you correctly, enforce daily limits, and prevent fraud.
  • Purchases (gem packs) and redemptions (converting your reward points into a mobile-money payout) are processed by Relworx, a licensed mobile-money payment aggregator. We share with Relworx only what the transaction needs: the mobile-money phone number you enter, the amount and the currency. Relworx's own privacy policy governs its processing.

3. Advertising

GridClan Puzzles shows advertisements from third-party advertising networks (up to three networks operating in a failover chain). These appear as optional "watch ad for rewards" ads and as popup ads after a game ends.

  • Ad networks may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), IP address, and coarse location to serve and measure ads, under their own privacy policies. We will list the active networks on this page once each integration goes live, with links to their policies.
  • Where the law requires consent for personalised advertising (e.g. the EU/UK), ads default to non-personalised unless you consent.
  • For users we know or believe to be minors, we request non-personalised, age-appropriate ads only and do not pass identifiers for ad personalisation.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings at any time. Buying any gem pack switches off popup ads for the period shown on the pack.

4. How long we keep data

  • Account data: while your account exists; erased within 30 days of a deletion request.
  • Points and gameplay audit records: retained in anonymised form after account deletion for integrity and anti-cheat purposes.
  • Purchase, redemption (payout), wallet and ad-reward records: retained as financial records for a minimum of 10 years, as required by Ugandan anti-money-laundering and financial record-keeping law (including the Anti-Money Laundering Act, 2013). These records survive account deletion: when your account is erased they are unlinked from your identity and kept under an anonymous reference, except that the mobile-money number on a payment or payout record is retained as part of the legally required transaction record.
  • Audit logs: 7 years.
  • IP addresses: purged automatically after 90 days.
  • Marketing consent records: until you withdraw consent.

5. Third parties we share data with

  • Relworx (payments and payouts), Supabase (database), Upstash (cache), Railway (hosting), Cloudflare (network), Netlify (web hosting), Resend (email), Firebase (push notifications), Sentry (crash reporting), and our third-party advertising networks (section 3).

We never sell your personal data. International transfers are covered by adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Your rights (all users)

  • Access & portability: download all your personal data as machine-readable JSON from Profile → Settings → Export My Data (or GET /user/data-export). We respond within 21 days.
  • Deletion: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. A 24-hour cancellation window applies; PII is then erased within 30 days. Points, gameplay and financial audit records are retained in anonymised form; financial transaction records (purchases, redemptions, wallet ledger, ad-reward records) are retained for the statutory period described in section 4 — this retention is a legal obligation and is not affected by a deletion request.
  • Rectification: edit your profile in the app or contact us.
  • Marketing consent: opt-in only; withdraw any time in Settings or via the unsubscribe link in any marketing email — it takes effect immediately.

7. Jurisdiction-specific rights

European Union / United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

The lawful bases above apply. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Data breaches notifiable to authorities are reported within 72 hours. Personalised advertising runs only with your consent.

Uganda (Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019)

GridClan Puzzles is registered with the Personal Data Protection Office and has appointed a Data Protection Officer. Requests are answered within 21 days.

California, USA (CCPA)

We do not sell personal information. A "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" control is available in Settings. Data requests are answered within 45 days.

Brazil (LGPD)

You hold the same access, deletion, and portability rights described in section 6; the legal basis for each processing activity is documented in section 1.

Children and teens (COPPA and equivalents)

Registration is blocked for anyone under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Players aged 13–17 may use GridClan Puzzles with a parent or guardian's consent (see the Terms of Service). For users we know or believe to be under 18 we request non-personalised, age-appropriate ads only, and we encourage parents to supervise any redemptions of reward points. Parents or guardians can contact us at any time to review or delete their teen's data.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; all traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.2+; access tokens are stored in hardware-backed secure storage on your device; we never log passwords, tokens, or personal identifiers. Wallet mutations are server-side only, row-locked, and fully audit-logged.
