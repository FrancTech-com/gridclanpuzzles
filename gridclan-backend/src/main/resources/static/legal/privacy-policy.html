GridClan Puzzles Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

GridClan Puzzles ("we", "us") operates the GridClan Puzzles free-to-play puzzle game platform. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to all users worldwide; jurisdiction-specific sections appear at the end.

1. Data we collect

We practise data minimisation: we do not collect your real name, home address, identity documents, or any payment information. GridClan Puzzles is a free-to-play game with no purchases, no cashouts, and no financial features.

2. How long we keep data

  • Account data: while your account exists; erased within 30 days of a deletion request.
  • Points and gameplay audit records: retained in anonymised form after account deletion for integrity and anti-cheat purposes.
  • Audit logs: 7 years.
  • IP addresses: purged automatically after 90 days.
  • Marketing consent records: until you withdraw consent.

3. Third parties we share data with

  • Supabase (database), Upstash (cache), Railway (hosting), Cloudflare (network), Resend (email), Firebase (push notifications), Sentry (crash reporting).

We never sell your personal data. International transfers are covered by adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses.

4. Your rights (all users)

  • Access & portability: download all your personal data as machine-readable JSON from Profile → Settings → Export My Data (or GET /user/data-export). We respond within 21 days.
  • Deletion: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. A 24-hour cancellation window applies; PII is then erased. Points and gameplay audit records are retained in anonymised form for game integrity.
  • Rectification: edit your profile in the app or contact us.
  • Marketing consent: opt-in only; withdraw any time in Settings or via the unsubscribe link in any marketing email — it takes effect immediately.

5. Jurisdiction-specific rights

European Union / United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

The lawful bases above apply. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Data breaches notifiable to authorities are reported within 72 hours.

Uganda (Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019)

GridClan Puzzles is registered with the Personal Data Protection Office and has appointed a Data Protection Officer. Requests are answered within 21 days.

California, USA (CCPA)

We do not sell personal information. A "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" control is available in Settings. Data requests are answered within 45 days.

Brazil (LGPD)

You hold the same access, deletion, and portability rights described in section 4; the legal basis for each processing activity is documented in section 1.

Children (COPPA and equivalents)

GridClan Puzzles is not directed at children. Registration is blocked for anyone under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. The game is not designed for children and carries an Everyone/PEGI 3 content rating.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt; all traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.2+; access tokens are stored in hardware-backed secure storage on your device; we never log passwords, tokens, or personal identifiers.
